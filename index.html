Types of Bets for Sports

We offer the following types of bets:

Single Bet A single outcome of an event. The winnings are equal to the sum of the bet and the odds.
Express This is a bet on several unrelated events. The bet wins if all outcomes are successful.
System This is a bet that consists of several express bets.
Chain A series of single bets on different outcomes. The sum of the bets on each single is equal to the sum of the chain.
Trust Rate A bet based on an estimate of the potential winnings on unfulfilled bets.
Promo Code Bet A bet with an individual promo code combination.
Multi Bet

Place a bet on several events at once, combining them into a single betting slip.
Conditional Bet

A combination of single and accumulator bets on different outcomes. The order of the outcome can be chosen by the user.
Anti-Accumulator

The bet must include two or more selections on unrelated events. The bet wins if at least one selection loses.
Lucky

Combines single bets and all possible accumulators for a specific number of selections, from 2 to 8.
Patent

Includes all possible accumulators from 3 to 8. To win, you need to guess at least one outcome correctly.
